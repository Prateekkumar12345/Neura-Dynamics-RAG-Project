Cancellation Policy

1. Order Cancellation
- Orders can be cancelled within 1 hour of purchase for a full refund.
- After 1 hour, orders enter processing and cannot be cancelled.
- Customers must contact customer support immediately for cancellation requests.

2. Subscription Cancellation
- Monthly subscriptions can be cancelled at any time from account settings.
- Access remains active until the end of the current billing cycle.
- No refunds are issued for monthly subscriptions after the billing date.

- Annual subscriptions can be cancelled at any time.
- Pro-rated refunds apply for unused months of annual subscriptions.

- Custom or made-to-order items cancelled after production begins incur a $25 cancellation fee.
